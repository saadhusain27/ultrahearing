About Us

Ultra Hearing Clinic is privately owned and operated by a team of Hearing Instrument Specialists dedicated to improving hearing and quality of life for our patients. Our experienced team of hearing specialists offers a range of services including comprehensive hearing tests, wax cleaning, as well as precise fitting and programming of advanced hearing aids. Experience personalized care and rediscover the joy of hearing with Ultra Hearing Clinic.

Why choose Ultra Hearing Clinic

At Ultra Hearing Clinic, We love helping people reconnect with their loved one through better hearing.

Our purpose is to help each of our clients achieve full spectrum hearing through a complete, high-quality hearing evaluation.

Our client satisfaction and hearing health is our top priority We walk with each client step by step throughout their hearing journey. We offer our client latest technology hearing aid which meets their needs personal preferences and lifestyle.

We can service and repair your hearing instruments in our office, often on a day’s notice. If your hearing device needs to be sent out for repair, we will be glad to expedite that service for you.

Hearing Instrument Specialist

Ayeesha Husain

A Honours Graduate in the Hearing Instrument Specialist Program from the Prestigious George Brown College, Ayeesha Husain also holds a Bachelor's degree in medicine from India. Having previously served as a Physician, she has rich experience in working with patients and providing them health care services. Her experience in hearing instruments includes conducting hearing evaluations, fitting and dispensing hearing aids, etc. Having worked with patients from different geographies and multicultural backgrounds, Ayeesha excels in providing counseling services and information regarding hearing loss to her clients and their family members.

Samah Hassanein

Samah Hassanein is an Honors Graduate in the Hearing Instrument Specialist Program at George Brown College. She has a Bachelor's degree in medicine, with over 15 years of experience in the hearing health sector and medical field. She has also worked as a physician outside Canada. Her personal journey as a parent to a child with hearing challenges has deepened her compassion and understanding for her clients. Her mission is clear: she is committed to helping people reconnect with their loved ones through improved hearing. She believes that hearing is about rediscovering life's cherished moments—the laughter of a grandchild, the warmth of a spouse's voice, and the beauty of nature. Samah excels in providing counselling and information about hearing loss to clients and their families.
