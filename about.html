About Us

Ultra hearing clinic is privately owned and operated by a team of Hearing Instrument specialists dedicated to improving hearing and quality of life for our patients. Our experienced team hearing specialists offers a range of services including comprehensive hearing tests, wax cleaning, as well as precise fitting and programming of advanced hearing aids. Experience personalized care and rediscover the joy of hearing with Ultra Hearing Clinic.

Ayeesha Husain

Hearing Instrument Specialist

A Honours Graduate in the Hearing Instrument Specialist Program from the Prestigious George Brown College, Ayeesha Husain also holds a Bachelor's degree in medicine from India. Having previously served as a Physician, she has rich experience in working with patients and providing them health care services. Her experience in hearing instruments includes conducting hearing evaluations, fitting and dispensing hearing aids, etc. Having worked with patients from different geographies and multicultural backgrounds, Ayeesha excels in providing counseling services and information regarding hearing loss to her clients and their family members.
