Types of Hearing Aids

Just as everyone is unique so is their ear anatomy. Hearing aids comes in different styles so that it fits individual needs. At Ultra Hearing Clinic, we choose high quality advanced hearing aids for our clients based on their level of hearing loss, lifestyle and budget.


There are 5 main types of hearing aids, all of which are available at Ultra Hearing Clinic.

In The Ear

ITE are custom made and fills the outer ear. Recommended for those with mild to severe hearing loss.

In The Canal

ITC are custom made partially fills the lower one half the concha. Recommended for those with moderately severe hearing loss.

Completely in the Canal

CIC are the smallest of the hearing aids which fits completely inside the ear canal. They are custom made and virtually invisible from outside. Recommended for those with mild to moderately severe hearing loss.

Behind the Ear

BTE is worn over the pinna and coupled with an ear mold or ear tip. Recommended for those with mild to profound hearing loss.

Receiver in the Canal

RIC are small, discreet and sit behind the pinna. Recommended for those with mild to moderately severe hearing loss.
