Frequently Asked Questioins

  • What are the signs of hearing loss?

    The most common signs of hearing loss are difficulty understanding speech especially in group or crowd, asking others to repeat themselves, turning the T.V volume up.

  • How long will it take for the Hearing test ?

    Depending upon the type and level of hearing loss it takes around 30-40 min .This include your general history ,examination of the ear ,hearing test and counselling.

  • Do we need referral from a doctor?

    Doctor referral is not needed. You can come as a walk in or book an appointment via phone or online.

  • What are the payment options?

    We accept cash, credit /debit card, cheque .We also offer different financing options to make hearing aid affordable for our clients.

  • Do you do home visits?

    Yes, we do home visit for clients who are unable to come to our clinic.
